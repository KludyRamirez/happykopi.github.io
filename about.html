About Us

Getting to know about our company


Our Company Profile

Happy Kopi is a 100% Filipino-owned company established in July 2022
A coffee shop that offers quality, delicious, yet affordable beverages such as Milk Tea, Coffee, and Fruit Tea.

With their expertise in franchising industry (Sister Company of Happy Grains Rice Business),
they apply their marketing skills and service experience thru Franchising that will give
opportunities to many Filipinos in the entire country to establish their own business.

Our Mission

To be the leading and most trusted coffee shop in promoting the quality yet affordable beverages and best services. To help and support Filipinos to start a new business that is easy to operate. We provide the most reasonable and affordable price. with the goal of giving opportunities most especially to those people with no business experience by helping them to establish their own business thru franchising.

Our Advantages

Affordable Products - Products are directly distributed from our commissary in Quezon City. After Sales Support- we have customer care team that you may contact anytime Marketing - We have 24/7 running ads on social media Supply Assurance-We have unlimited supplies and stocks Service experience - Sister company of the top leading company in Rice franchising (Happy Grains)
